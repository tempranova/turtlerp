Features

TurtleRP is heavily inspired by Total RP 3, and derives many features from it. We are looking for more ideas, so get in touch anytime!

Player Icons

Choose an icon for your character that will show up when players mouse over you.

Custom Names

Create a title, first name, and last name for your character.

Enhanced Tooltip

See class colors, guild affiliation, custom icons, IC and OOC information all in the tooltip!

Stored Data

Data for all characters is cached and stored for quick access and future character directories.

Descriptions

Provide descriptions in plain text or WoW's allowed basic HTML.

At A Glance

Select icons and snippets to display whenever players target you.

About

There's just no good RP addons out there for Vanilla. MyRolePlay and ImmersionRP have too many bugs and not enough features, and we're spoiled by the awesomeness of addons like TRP3.

So, Vee (Drixi in-game), in coordination with Ashkir, decided to get working on a new addon, custom-built specifically for Vanilla servers.

After an obsessive few weeks of work, the result is TurtleRP!

  • Communication: To share information between players, TurtleRP uses the TTRP channel. Information is cached and pre-checked for changes.
  • Icons: TurtleRP refers only to icons included in Vanilla WoW, although we are open to adding more in the future.
  • Descriptions: Descriptions use the plain text and basic HTML that Blizzard frames allow.
  • Storage: All player information related in the TTRP channel is stored by all players, to reduce chat spam.

See the Donate section for planned features.

Help

Addon isn't working? Here are a few things to test.

  • TTRP Channel: You must be in the TTRP channel to share descriptions, tooltip information, etc, with other players. Right-click on the "General" chat tab, hover over channels, and see if the TTRP channel is listed there. If not, /join ttrp, then unclick it from the Channels menu (so you don't have to see the channel spam).
  • Low Level: On the TurtleRP server, you can't speak before level 5. This affects communication with other players, so you're only able to see your own profile yourself before level 5.
  • Errors: If you're running into any interface errors or alerts, please take a screenshot and send it to us.
  • Other: Take a screenshot of your error and send it to us. Thanks!
